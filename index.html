PROJECTS

  • Incremental FlashFill [In Progress]

    • FlashFill is a Microsoft Excel 2013 feature that fills out columns of the spreadsheet automatically when a pattern is visible based on a few examples provided by the user. The work is explained in Automating String Processing in Spreadsheets using Input-Output Examples, POPL 2011, Sumit Gulwani. We are working on incrementalizing the algorithm.

  • Predicting and Mapping the Northern Lights

    • Northern Lights (also known as Aurora Borealis) are natural electromagnetic phenomena characterized by the appearance of colored lights in the night sky, usually near the higher latitudes. Using a historical dataset obtained from a network of magnetometers spread across the western hemisphere between the years 1997 and 2015, a supervised machine learning model is trained to predict the intensity of auroral activity on a given day. [technical report]

  • Predicting betrayals in Diplomacy, an online strategy game

    • We extended the work done in the paper - Vlad Niculae, Srijan Kumar, Jordan L. Boyd-Graber, and Cristian Danescu-Niculescu-Mizil. 2015. Linguistic Harbingers of Betrayal: A Case Study on an Online Strategy Game. CoRR abs/1506.04744 (2015) to include game states along with linguistic cues to predict betrayal in Diplomacy. [video]
