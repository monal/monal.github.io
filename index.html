COURSE PROJECTS

  • Predicting and Mapping the Northern Lights

    • Northern Lights (also known as Aurora Borealis) are natural electromagnetic phenomena characterized by the appearance of colored lights in the night sky, usually near the higher latitudes. Using a historical dataset obtained from a network of magnetometers spread across the western hemisphere between the years 1997 and 2015, a supervised machine learning model is trained to predict the intensity of auroral activity on a given day.
